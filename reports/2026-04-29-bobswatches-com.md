## 5. Recommended Actions for Wrist Aficionado

### Priority 1: Strengthen Existing Rolex Model Pages for Pre-Owned and Used Commercial Intent

Wrist Aficionado already has `/collections/rolex-submariner`, `/collections/rolex-daytona`, `/collections/rolex-datejust`, and `/collections/rolex-gmt-master-ii`. Bob's Watches is dominating the "used" and "pre-owned" modifier space at position 1 for every high-volume Rolex term with these exact model pages.

**Action:** Update the existing pages listed above to explicitly target pre-owned and used intent in title tags, H1s, and above-the-fold copy. Add a concise editorial section to each page covering current market context, what to look for in a pre-owned example, and reference pricing. This is the single highest-leverage move given Bob's current dominance of that SERP cluster.

### Priority 2: Expand the Existing Rolex Daytona Blog Content into a Stronger Commercial Hub

Wrist Aficionado has `/blogs/news/rolex-daytona-116508-collector-s-guide` and `/blogs/news/the-ultimate-rolex-panda-daytona-buyer-s-guide-in-2025` and the collection page `/collections/rolex-daytona`. Bob's Watches holds position 2 for "rolex daytona" (90,500 monthly searches) through a dedicated model hub at `bobswatches.com/rolex-daytona-1.html`.

**Action:** Internally link both existing Daytona blog posts directly into the `/collections/rolex-daytona` collection page. Add a brief editorial summary at the top of that collection page connecting buyer intent to collector context. The goal is to consolidate the editorial authority already earned by the blog posts into the commercial landing page, narrowing the gap with Bob's position 2 ranking.

### Priority 3: Strengthen the Existing Rolex Datejust Collection Page for "Used" and "Pre-Owned" Intent

Wrist Aficionado already has `/collections/rolex-datejust` (and the sub-collection `/collections/rolex-datejust-two-tone`). Bob's Watches holds position 1 for "used rolex datejust" (9,900 monthly searches) with a dedicated Datejust hub at `bobswatches.com/rolex-datejust-1.html`. The opportunity is not a new build, it is closing the SERP gap on existing real estate.

**Action:** Audit `/collections/rolex-datejust` for the same pre-owned and used commercial intent gap identified in Priority 1. Specifically: ensure the title tag and H1 explicitly target "used Rolex Datejust," "pre-owned Rolex Datejust," and "Rolex Datejust for sale." Add a short buying guide section above the product grid covering size variants (36mm vs 41mm), bezel types (smooth, fluted, diamond), and two-tone vs steel considerations. Cross-link `/collections/rolex-datejust-two-tone` as a sub-collection from the main page. Internally link any existing Datejust blog content into this page to consolidate topical authority.

### Priority 4: Build Out Cartier Tank Sub-Variants and Roadster as Genuine Content Gaps

Wrist Aficionado has 11 Cartier collection pages: `/collections/cartier`, `/collections/cartier-tank` (parent only), `/collections/cartier-santos`, `/collections/cartier-ballon-bleu`, `/collections/cartier-baignoire`, `/collections/cartier-crash`, `/collections/panthere-de-cartier`, `/collections/pasha-de-cartier`, plus three location-tagged pages (Beverly Hills, Miami, New York City).

Bob's Watches has built dedicated sub-pages for every Tank variant (Louis, Francaise, Americaine, Must, Solo) and other models (Roadster, Calibre, Rotonde, vintage). Wrist Aficionado is genuinely missing dedicated collection pages for:
- Cartier Tank Francaise
- Cartier Tank Americaine
- Cartier Tank Louis Cartier
- Cartier Tank Must
- Cartier Tank Solo
- Cartier Roadster

**Action (build new):** Create dedicated collection pages for Tank Francaise and Tank Americaine first, since these are the highest-volume Tank sub-variants in commercial search ("Cartier Tank Francaise for sale," "Cartier Tank Americaine pre-owned"). Tank Louis Cartier is the next priority for collector-tier traffic. Roadster is a smaller win but a clear gap.

**Action (strengthen existing):** Audit `/collections/cartier-tank`, `/collections/cartier-santos`, `/collections/cartier-baignoire`, `/collections/pasha-de-cartier`, and the other existing Cartier pages for editorial depth. For any page that is currently a bare product grid, add 150 to 250 words of model context (history, reference numbers, sizing, what to look for in pre-owned).

### Priority 5: Create a Rolex Serial Numbers Resource Page

Bob's Watches has `bobswatches.com/rolex-serial-numbers` cited twice by the Wikipedia Rolex article (Authority Score 61) and linked from a pawn shop domain. This single resource page is driving both backlink authority and informational traffic that reinforces brand trust across the entire site.

Wrist Aficionado has `/blogs/news/the-ultimate-guide-to-rolex-reference-numbers-in-2025` which covers reference numbers but does not appear to be a dedicated serial number lookup or production date resource. These are distinct searches with distinct intent.

**Action:** Build a standalone resource page (e.g., `/pages/rolex-serial-numbers-guide` or a long-form blog post titled as a serial number guide) covering Rolex serial number ranges by year, production date lookup, and how to use serial numbers to authenticate a watch. Structure it as a reference tool, not a standard blog post. Submit it for potential inclusion in watch-related Wikipedia citations over time.

### Priority 6: Develop a Breitling Brand and Model Presence

Wrist Aficionado has no Breitling pages in the existing content list at all. Bob's Watches has a Breitling brand hub plus over 30 Breitling model sub-pages covering Navitimer, Chronomat, Superocean, Avenger, Bentley editions, Premier, and more.

Breitling is not in Wrist Aficionado's current brand mix, which is concentrated on ultra-luxury. This may be a deliberate positioning choice. However, the Navitimer and Chronomat are legitimate collector-tier pieces that appear in the same secondary market conversations as Rolex GMT and Submariner.

**Action:** Evaluate whether Breitling fits the Wrist Aficionado positioning. If yes, start with a single brand hub page (`/collections/breitling`) and two model pages for Navitimer and Chronomat, which carry the most collector credibility. If Breitling is outside the intended brand scope, skip this entirely and note it as a known gap.
